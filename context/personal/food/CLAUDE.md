# Food

Everything about what the user eats — rules, cooking, shopping, and dining out.

## What lives here

- `allowed/` — dietary rules, allergies, and preferences (the master filter)
- `eating-out/` — favorite restaurants and dining notes
- `groceries/` — shopping lists, staples, and pantry inventory
- `recipies/` — saved recipes with ingredients and steps

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- `allowed/` is the authoritative filter — every suggestion must pass it first.
- Treat allergies as hard blocks; preferences as soft ranking signals.
