# Car Records

The legal and document trail for the vehicle.

## What lives here

- `registration/` — registration document and expiry
- `title/` — legal title, ownership, and lien status
- `warranties/` — active warranties and their coverage windows

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Keep registration, title, and inspection expiries distinct — they are different dates.
- Treat documents as the source of truth; flag anything expired or branded.
