# Car General

Baseline facts about the vehicle: who it is and how far it's gone.

## What lives here

- `info/` — make, model, year, VIN, plate, and color
- `milage/` — dated odometer log and mileage averages

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- `info/` holds identity; `milage/` holds the time series — keep them separate.
- Always note the unit (miles vs km) on every mileage reading.
