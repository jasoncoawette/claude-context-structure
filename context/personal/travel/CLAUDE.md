# Travel

The user's trips: flights and time away from work.

## What lives here

- `airlines/` — preferred airlines, loyalty programs, booking prefs
- `ooo/` — out-of-office planning and coverage

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Confirm dates, times, and timezones before booking.
- Cross-check trip dates against the user's OOO and other commitments.
