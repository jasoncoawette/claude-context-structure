# Personal

The user's personal life: everything outside of work.

## What lives here

- `car/` — vehicle, maintenance, registration, insurance
- `dating/` — date ideas and scheduling
- `finance/` — personal budgeting, accounts, bills
- `food/` — meals, recipes, groceries, dining
- `health/` — appointments, fitness, insurance, medical records
- `home/` — chores, internet, rent, household logistics
- `social/` — friends, events, family relationships
- `travel/` — airlines, out-of-office and trip planning

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Treat all data here as private; never share it outside the user's own tools.
- Dates use ISO format (YYYY-MM-DD); the user's timezone is the default.
