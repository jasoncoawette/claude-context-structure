# Finance

The user's personal financial picture — accounts, spending, credit, investing, and taxes.

## What lives here

- `accounts/` — bank and financial accounts with balances
- `budgets/` — monthly category budgets vs actuals
- `credit-score/` — score history and contributing factors
- `investments/` — holdings, allocation, and performance
- `recurring/` — subscriptions and recurring bills
- `taxes/` — tax-related records and deductions

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Always cite the as-of date for any balance, price, or score — values go stale.
- Expose only the minimum account detail needed (e.g., last four digits).
