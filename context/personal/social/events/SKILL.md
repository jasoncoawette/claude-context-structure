# Social Events

## What this does

Manages the user's social calendar: parties, dinners, gatherings — tracking RSVPs, dates, and what to bring or prepare.

## When to use

- The user is invited to an event and needs to RSVP or log it
- The user is planning or hosting a gathering
- The user asks what's on their social calendar

## Process

1. Capture the event date, location, host, and RSVP status.
2. Check for calendar conflicts before confirming.
3. Note any prep (gift, dish, outfit) and set a reminder.

## Common pitfalls

- Double-booking the same evening.
- Forgetting to RSVP or to bring what was agreed.

- `Script.py` — list upcoming events with RSVP status and dates.
