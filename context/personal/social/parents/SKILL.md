# Parents

## What this does

Keeps the user connected with their parents: tracks last call/visit, important dates, and nudges regular check-ins.

## When to use

- It's been a while since the user spoke with their parents
- A parent's birthday, anniversary, or holiday is coming up
- The user wants to plan a visit or call

## Process

1. Check when the user last called or visited.
2. Note any upcoming dates worth acknowledging.
3. Suggest a call or visit and update last-contact after.

## Common pitfalls

- Letting weeks slip by without a check-in.
- Forgetting birthdays, anniversaries, or holidays.

## Scripts

- `Script.py` — report days since last contact and any upcoming family dates.
