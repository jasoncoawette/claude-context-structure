# Social

The user's relationships: friends, family, and events.

## What lives here

- `close-circle/` — closest friends the user prioritizes
- `cool-people/` — acquaintances and people worth staying in touch with
- `events/` — gatherings, parties, and social calendar
- `parents/` — staying connected with the user's parents

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Remember names, context, and last-contact dates for each person.
- Be warm but never invent details about people you don't have data for.
