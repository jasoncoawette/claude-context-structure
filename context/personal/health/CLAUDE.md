# Health

The user's health: medical care, fitness, insurance, and records.

## What lives here

- `appointments/` — scheduling and tracking doctor/dentist visits
- `fitness/` — workouts and gym routine
- `insurnace/` — health insurance plan, coverage, claims
- `records/` — medical history, test results, prescriptions

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Medical data is sensitive; keep it within the user's private tools.
- Always confirm dates and provider names before booking or changing anything.
