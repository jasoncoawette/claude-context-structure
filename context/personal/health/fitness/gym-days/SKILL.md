# Gym Days

## What this does

Keeps the user's weekly gym schedule and training split organized, and helps plan which days to train versus rest.

## When to use

- The user asks which muscle group or workout is on for today
- The user wants to plan or shuffle the week's gym days
- The user missed a session and needs to rebalance

## Process

1. Check the user's current split and which days are assigned to what.
2. Account for rest/recovery days and any schedule conflicts.
3. Propose the day's workout or an updated weekly plan.

## Common pitfalls

- Scheduling the same muscle group on consecutive days.
- Ignoring rest days, leading to overtraining.

## Scripts

- `Script.py` — print today's planned workout based on the weekly split.
