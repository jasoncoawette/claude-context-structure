# Date Ideas

## What this does

A curated bank of date ideas the user can pull from, matched to budget, vibe, weather, and what's known about the person.

## When to use

- The user is planning a date and wants suggestions
- An existing plan falls through and needs a backup
- The user wants something fresh beyond the usual spots

## Process

1. Clarify budget, vibe, time of day, and any details about the person.
2. Filter the idea bank by those constraints and the weather.
3. Offer a few options with a suggested time and rough cost.

## Common pitfalls

- Suggesting ideas that ignore stated budget or dietary needs.
- Recommending outdoor plans without checking the forecast.

## Scripts

- `Script.py` — pick random date ideas filtered by budget and vibe tags.
