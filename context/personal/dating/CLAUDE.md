# Dating

The user's dating life: planning dates and keeping track of them.

## What lives here

- `date-ideas/` — a bank of date ideas to pull from
- `date-schedule/` — upcoming and past dates on the calendar

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Keep this private and discreet.
- Match ideas to the user's stated preferences, budget, and the person.
