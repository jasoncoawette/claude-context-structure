# Rent

## What this does

Keeps the user's rent and lease details on hand: monthly amount, due date, payment method, and landlord contact, with timely payment reminders.

## When to use

- Rent is coming due and needs a reminder or payment
- The user asks about lease terms or renewal dates
- The user needs the landlord's contact info

## Process

1. Confirm the monthly amount, due date, and payment method.
2. Remind ahead of the due date and confirm once paid.
3. Track lease end and renewal windows.

## Common pitfalls

- Missing the due date or any late-fee grace period.
- Losing track of when the lease renews or auto-renews.

## Scripts

- `Script.py` — report rent amount, next due date, and days remaining.
