# Home

The user's household: chores, utilities, and housing logistics.

## What lives here

- `chores/` — recurring household tasks (dishes, laundry)
- `internet/` — home internet provider, plan, and outages
- `rent/` — monthly rent, lease, and landlord contact

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Recurring tasks have a cadence; track last-done dates to avoid drift.
- Bills are time-sensitive; surface due dates early.
