# Chores

Recurring household tasks the user keeps on a rhythm.

## What lives here

- `dishes/` — washing and kitchen cleanup cadence
- `laundry/` — wash, dry, fold routine and schedule

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Each chore has an expected frequency; flag when it's overdue.
- Keep instructions practical and low-friction.
