# Business

Everything related to running the user's business: content, design, engineering, and operations.

## What lives here

- `content/` — content creation, publishing, and editorial work
- `design/` — brand, visual, and product design assets and guidelines
- `engineering/` — software, infrastructure, and technical build work
- `operations/` — day-to-day running of the business: CRM, support, finance, research, prioritization

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Identify the single domain a task belongs to before acting; split cross-domain work.
- Treat business data (customer, financial) as sensitive — never expose it outside the relevant tool or task.
