# Creation

## What this does

Takes a single idea from a rough concept to a polished, publish-ready piece — outline, draft, edit — anchored to one clear message and audience.

## When to use

- Starting a new article, post, video, or other standalone piece
- Turning a raw idea or brief into a finished draft
- Editing a draft up to a publishable bar

## Process

1. Pin down the single core idea, audience, and desired action.
2. Outline the structure before writing the full draft.
3. Draft, then edit for clarity, voice, and accuracy.
4. Finalize the hook/title and confirm it's ready to hand off to `scale/`.

## Common pitfalls

- Cramming multiple ideas into one piece, diluting the message.
- Polishing prose before the structure is settled.
- Skipping a strong hook, so good content goes unread.

- `Script.py` — scaffold a piece from a brief with an outline and metadata stub.
