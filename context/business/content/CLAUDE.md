# Content

Everything about producing, multiplying, and timing published content.

## What lives here

- `creation/` — drafting and producing individual content pieces
- `scale/` — repurposing one piece into many across channels
- `schedule/` — planning and timing the publishing calendar

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree. Typical flow: `creation/` -> `scale/` -> `schedule/`.

## Conventions

- One core idea per piece; everything else is a derivative produced in `scale/`.
- Nothing publishes without a slot on the calendar in `schedule/`.
