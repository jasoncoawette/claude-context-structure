# Scale

## What this does

Takes a single finished piece from `creation/` and multiplies it into channel-native derivatives — threads, clips, snippets, newsletters — without rewriting from scratch.

## When to use

- A piece is finished and ready to be distributed widely
- You need channel-specific cuts of one source asset
- Maximizing reach from existing content rather than making new content

## Process

1. Confirm the source piece is final; pull out its key points and quotes.
2. Map each target channel to a derivative format and length.
3. Adapt tone, format, and CTA per channel — don't just copy-paste.
4. Hand the batch of derivatives to `schedule/` for slotting.

## Common pitfalls

- Posting the same text verbatim across every platform.
- Scaling a weak piece — fix it in `creation/` first.
- Losing the core message in the reformat.

## Scripts

- `Script.py` — split one source piece into per-channel derivative drafts.
