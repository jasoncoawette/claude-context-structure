# Taxes

Tax preparation, filing support, and deduction tracking for the business.

## What lives here

- `deductions/` — identifying, categorizing, and substantiating deductible expenses

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Use the applicable tax year's rules; note the year on every calculation.
- Substantiation matters — tie each deduction to a receipt, invoice, or statement.
