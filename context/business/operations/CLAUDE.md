# Operations

Day-to-day running of the business: customer relationships, support, finance, research, and prioritization.

## What lives here

- `crm/` — managing contacts, leads, and the sales pipeline
- `customer-support/` — handling customer questions, issues, and escalations
- `deep-resarch/` — multi-source, fact-checked research on business questions
- `finance/` — financial records, taxes, and deductions
- `highest-leverage-tasks/` — finding and ranking the work that moves the needle most
- `ltv-to-cac/` — calculating and tracking customer lifetime value vs. acquisition cost

## How to navigate

Read the relevant leaf `SKILL.md` for task instructions; don't load the whole tree.

## Conventions

- Quantify when possible — operations decisions should rest on numbers, not vibes.
- Confirm before any action that writes to external systems (CRM, email, accounting).
