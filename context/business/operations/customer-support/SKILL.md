# Customer Support

## What this does

Triages incoming customer messages, drafts clear and on-brand replies, resolves common issues, and escalates anything that needs a human or a refund decision.

## When to use

- A customer email, ticket, or message needs a reply.
- An issue must be triaged by urgency and routed to the right owner.
- A recurring complaint should be summarized for the team.

## Process

1. Read the full thread and identify the customer's actual ask and sentiment.
2. Check known issues and prior resolutions before drafting a response.
3. Draft a reply; resolve directly if routine, escalate with context if not.

## Common pitfalls

- Answering the literal question while missing the underlying frustration.
- Promising refunds, fixes, or timelines outside your authority.
- Closing a ticket without confirming the customer's issue is resolved.

## Scripts

- `Script.py` — pull open tickets, tag by topic and urgency, and draft first-response templates.
